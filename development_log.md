**Week 1**
**Task 1 pseudocode**
START 
 
1. Fill kettle with water 
2. Turn kettle on 
3. Wait until water boils 
4. Get a cup 
5. Add coffee powder to cup 
 
6. IF sugar is desired THEN 
  Add sugar 
END IF 
 
7. Pour boiled water into cup 
8. Stir with spoon 
9. Serve coffee 
 
END
